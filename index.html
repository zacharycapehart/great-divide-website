Our Story

Rooted in Community, Driven by Flavor

Great Divide started as a simple idea: bring people together over honest, delicious food made with ingredients sourced from nearby farms and purveyors.

Our kitchen team changes the menu every few weeks to follow the seasons, so there is always something new to discover — and something comfortably familiar to return to.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone or grabbing a weeknight dinner, we make every table feel like the best seat in the house.
