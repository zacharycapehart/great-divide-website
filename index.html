Our Story

Rooted in Community, Driven by Season

Great Divide was born from a simple idea: that the best meals come from knowing where your food originates. We partner directly with family farms within 100 miles of our kitchen to bring the freshest produce, meats, and dairy to your table.

Our menu evolves every few weeks as harvests change. What you taste in spring will be different from what you taste in autumn — and that's exactly the point.
