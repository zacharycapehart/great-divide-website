Music in the Pub

Live Music

Most evenings, there is music. A piano player on a Tuesday. A local duo on Friday. Occasionally something you have never heard of that turns out to be exactly right for the room. The music at The Great Divide fills the space without taking it over — you can still hear the person across the table, which is how it should be in a pub. The performers are local, the sets are relaxed, and nobody is charging a cover.

Every Other Tuesday

Jazz Night. A relaxed neighborhood set that still leaves room for conversation.

Thursday – Friday

Live piano. Low-key, familiar, and easy to talk over.
