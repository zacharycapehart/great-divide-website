Music in the Pub

Live Music

Live music is part of what makes The Great Divide feel like it does. Most evenings you will find a piano player or a local musician adding to the atmosphere without turning the room into a concert hall. It is music in a pub — the way it should be.

Tuesday – Thursday

Piano nights. Low-key, familiar, and easy to talk over.

Friday – Saturday

Local musicians and small ensembles. Good variety, always neighborhood-friendly.

Sunday

Occasional acoustic sets. Check back or call ahead for the current schedule.

Current Schedule

The live music schedule is updated regularly. For the most current lineup, call us at (317) 637-2192 or stop in and ask.
